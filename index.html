The Challenge

Why Eva-Safe Exists

Disasters are unpredictable. Without the right tools, finding safety and staying in touch with loved ones becomes a major challenge.

Difficulty Finding Centers

Finding the nearest evacuation center often feels like guesswork in high-stress situations.

Losing Contact

Families often get separated and lose contact when communication networks are strained.

Limited Real-time Info

Relying on outdated news or social media leads to confusion during emergencies.

Lack of Unified Tools

Most safety apps lack a combination of mapping, family tracking, and SOS features.

What We Offer

Core Features

Everything you need in one unified platform — built for speed, reliability, and ease-of-use when it matters most.

Evacuation Map

Real-time navigation to nearest safe zones with live capacity updates and route guidance.

Family Tracking

Offline-first family location sharing with emergency contact alerts and status updates.

Real-time Alerts

Push notifications with severity levels and actionable instructions delivered instantly.

SOS Button

One-tap emergency signal with auto location sharing directly to first responders.
