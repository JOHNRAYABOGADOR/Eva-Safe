Challenges We Solve

The critical gaps in disaster response and family safety

Finding Evacuation Centers

People cannot quickly locate nearby safe zones during emergencies, wasting critical time.

Losing Family Contact

Communication networks collapse, separating families with no way to know if loved ones are safe.

Delayed Emergency Info

Critical alerts and updates reach people too late, reducing response effectiveness and safety.

Fragmented Solutions

Multiple apps for tracking, mapping, and alerts create confusion and data silos.
